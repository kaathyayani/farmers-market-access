Welcome

This mobile app was developed as part of the Smart India Hackathon. It connects farmers directly to consumers, retailers, and businesses to sell their produce at fair prices, eliminating middlemen and increasing profit margins.

Problem & Solution

Problem: Farmers face losses due to the involvement of middlemen, price manipulation, and limited market access.

Solution: Our app allows farmers to list their crops and buyers to contact them directly, ensuring transparent and fair transactions with live chat, order booking, and secure tracking.

Features

✔ Farmer & Buyer Login
Separate modules for secure access
✔ Crop Listings
Post available produce with pricing
✔ Real-Time Chat
Communicate directly within the app
✔ Order Management
Track and fulfill buyer orders
✔ Admin Dashboard
Moderate users and resolve issues
✔ Data Analytics
Market price comparison for fair pricing
