Welcome

This app was developed for the Smart India Hackathon. It connects farmers directly to consumers and retailers, helping them earn better by removing middlemen.

Problem & Solution

Problem: Farmers face losses due to middlemen, price manipulation, and no direct access to markets.

Solution: Our app allows farmers to post their crops and buyers to contact them directly.

Features

  • ✔ Farmer & Buyer Login
  • ✔ Crop Listings
  • ✔ In-App Chat
  • ✔ Order Management
  • ✔ Admin Dashboard
  • ✔ Market Price Comparison
